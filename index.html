About Me

Bridging Technology & Agriculture

RT

A Passionate Professional Driven by Purpose

I'm Robertus Theodore, a professional with a well-rounded background in technology, governance, and entrepreneurship. My career has been guided by a deep belief in the power of Information and Communication Technologies (ICTs) to drive positive change in competitiveness, economic growth, and social development.

With a Bachelor of Engineering in Informatics from Bandung Institute of Technology (ITB) and experience spanning the World Bank, the Executive Office of the President of Indonesia, and now leading my own agribusiness ventures, I've dedicated my career to creating meaningful impact.

"Growing up watching farmers work tirelessly in the fields, I've always been inspired by the essential role they play in feeding millions. Indonesia was once an agricultural kingdom — my mission is to help it thrive again."

Philosophy

What Drives Me

Indonesia was once an agricultural kingdom. Before skyscrapers and startups, our archipelago thrived on rice terraces, volcanic soil, and farmers who understood the rhythm of the seasons. My mission is to help it prosper once again.

Empowerment

Creating systems that give farmers the tools, knowledge, and market access they need to thrive independently.

Innovation

Leveraging technology and data to solve age-old agricultural challenges with modern, scalable solutions.

Sustainability

Building food and nutrition security for all layers of community while preserving our environment for future generations.
